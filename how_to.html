How to make Juice at Home

  1. Prepare your fruits and vegetables by gathering and washing them.
  2. Cut the fruits and vegetables to the appropriate size.
  3. Mix the fruits and vegetables.
  4. Adds spices and flavorings.
  5. Enjoy!
