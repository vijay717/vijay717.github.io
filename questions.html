Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, all of our juices are gluten-free.

3. What does it mean to be "Non-GMO"

All of our juices are Non-GMO. This means we don't use any genetically modified organisms in our juices.

Yes, our juices contain no animal products.

Yes, gift cards are available in any amount at every Jumpin' Juice store.
